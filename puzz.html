The Thinker's Playground

Test your mathematical intuition against these classic cerebral challenges.

Pure Logic

The False Coin Dilemma

You have 9 identical-looking coins. One is counterfeit and weighs slightly less than the others. What is the minimum number of weighings needed on a balance scale to find it?

Visual Geometry

The Infinite Square Path

Imagine a square of area 1. You cut it in half. You cut that half in half, and repeat infinitely. If you sum all the infinitely small pieces together, what total area do you get?

Probability

The Rice Grain Paradox

A chessboard starts with 1 grain of rice on the first square, 2 on the second, 4 on the third, doubling each time. How many grains are on the 7th square?
